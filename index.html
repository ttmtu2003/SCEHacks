    Overview of Housing Crisis in California

    The current housing market in California is similar to how it was prior to COVID. Sale prices in many regions have been rapidly increasing within the past few years. Over last year, median sale price increased by $100,000, averaging above $800,000 in areas such as Irvine, Fremont, Oxnard, Elk Grove, and San Jose [1].
